Five Pillars of ISLAM

Other Four pillars

Iman is mainly from believe. Salah,Sawm ,Zakat & Hajj are mainly physical.

Salah

Salah (Arabic: صَلاة, pl salawat, lit. 'prayer'), also known as namāz and also spelled salat, are prayers performed by Muslims

Zakat

Zakat (Arabic: زكاة, Zakah) is a form of almsgiving to the Muslim Ummah treated in Islam as a religious obligation, which, by Quranic ranking, is next after prayer (salat) in importance.

Sawm

Sawm(Fasting in Islam,Arabic: صَوْم Siyam, also commonly known as Rōzah) is the practice of abstaining, usually from food, drink, smoking, and sexual activities.

The Hajj

The Hajj (Arabic: حَجّ) is an annual Islamic pilgrimage to Mecca, Saudi Arabia,the holiest city for Muslims.
