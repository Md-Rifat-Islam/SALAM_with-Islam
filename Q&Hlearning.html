How to Learn Quran Fast?

Learning the Quran takes time, and time is precious. So the solution is to make the most of your time by speeding up the learning process itself.

Quran Ayat’s experts & teachers came up with 5 tips to learn Quran faster.

1. Clear Your Mind

Before start taking your online Quran classes, clear your mind from any stress or unwanted thoughts, and keep all your focus towards your goal (learning the Quran);The mind can only really think of one thing at a time.

2. Prepare The Learning Environment

Make sure to sit in a quiet place while taking your classes in order not to get distracted by the affairs of people surrounding you. Also, make sure that your device (PC – Laptop – Tablet – Smart Phone) that you’ll use to take your class is connected to the internet properly and have the online classroom app installed to it.

3. Take Notes With Pen & Paper

While it’s faster to take notes on a laptop or mobile during the session, using a pen & paper will help you learn and comprehend better. Researches confirmed that although taking notes by hand is slower and more cumbersome than typing on a computer or mobile, the act of writing out the information fosters comprehension and retention.

4. Engage With Your Teacher & Ask Questions

Although we should listen twice more than we speak. Still, speaking and asking questions is a very important part of meaningful learning. Accordingly, you should listen to your teacher but also engage with him/her and ask all questions and inquiries you have in mind to better understand your lessons.

5. Learn Through Short Bursts Of Time

It has been proven that learning through short-sessions is much better than longer-sessions. The ideal single session duration is 30 – 60 minutes. In other words, while 30 minutes is the minimum time to get a proper amount of knowledge and save it to memory, more than 60 minutes is considered too much information for your brain can take in at one time.

How To Learn Quran Easy?

So, if you or your kids want to learn the Quran easily, Quran experts & tutors at Quran Ayat Institute got you covered. They broke down the Quran study into step-by-step courses that take you or your kids from the beginner level to mastering the Quran and even teaching it to others.

STEP 1: Learn To Read The Quran

To learn the Quran, you should firstly start with learning the basics of Quran reading. Learn how Arabic letters are linked together or separated. And how to read words, then short sentences and long sentences.

During the “Reading Quran Basics” Program at Quran Ayat, You will learn:

  • Quran Phonetics
  • The Arabic Alphabet
  • Arabic Diacritics
  • Letters Forms
  • Vowels
  • Sukun
  • Shaddah
  • Madd – Lengthening
  • Shaddah with Tanween
  • Laam rules
  • AlifulWasl rules
  • Characteristics of the Quranic writing

STEP 2: Learn To Recite The Quran

Once you have learned the basics of reading the Quran, it’s time to learn how to recite the Quran in a more professional way as reported by the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH).

During the “Quran Recitation” Program at Quran Ayat, You will learn:

  • Pronunciation of Every Alphabet
  • Pronunciation of Every Word
  • Different Ways of Recitation
  • Ayat (Verses) Meanings
  • Characteristics of the Quranic writing
  • Reciting The Whole Quran Fluently

STEP 3: Learn Tajweed Rules

To master the recitation of the Quran, one needs to excel in “Tajweed”. Tajweed (The grammar of the Quran) constitutes the rules and regulations one needs to follow in recitation to make it beautiful and accurate.

During the “Quran Tajweed” Program at Quran Ayat, You will learn:

  • What is Tajweed
  • Benefits of learning Tajweed
  • Heavy and light letters
  • Rules of Noon Saakin and Tanween
  • Rules of Meem Saakin
  • Qalqala
  • Laam in the exalted name of Allah
  • Letter Raa rules
  • Madd – Lengthening rules
  • Stopping at the end of words
  • The Qualities of the letters

STEP 4: Learn To Memorize The Quran

After mastering the Quran Recitation with Tajweed, you can now proceed with memorizing the Quran and having the outstanding power and strength of reciting the Quran by heart. Becoming a Hafiz/Hafiza is a great deal in Islam at both this life and hereafter.

During the “Quran Memorization” Program at Quran Ayat, You will learn:

  • Accurate Memorization of The Book of Allah (Quran)
  • Techniques For Fast Memorization
  • The Forgetfulness Types
  • How To Overcome Forgetfulness
  • The General Meaning of The Verses
  • The Reasons For Revealing The Verses
  • Advantages of Memorizing The Quran

STEP 5: Get Ijazah & Start Teaching Quran

The Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said:

The best among you (Muslims) are those who learn the Qur’an and teach it.

Sahih al-Bukhari 5027

“Ijazah”  means that you master Quran Recitation with Tajweed or Quran Memorization and can start teaching the Quran.  One can get Ijazah from a certified scholar for teaching recitation or memorization of the Quran to other people.

During the “Quran Ijazah” Program at Quran Ayat, You will learn:

  • Quran Recitation
  • Quran Tajweed Rules
  • The Sounds, Articulation, and Characteristics
  • Quran Memorization
  • Hadith
  • Quran Tafseer
